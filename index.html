Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12 July 2026

Introduction

Thank you for using Devashmi Cash Tool . This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

Devashmi Cash Tool does not directly collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Financial account details
  • Identity documents

Cash, GST, and discount calculations are performed locally on your device.

2. Calculation History

The application may save recent calculations locally on your device for your convenience.

Calculation history is not uploaded to a Devashmi server and is not shared with us.

You can clear saved calculation history from within the application.

3. Advertisements

Devashmi Cash Tool uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google and its advertising partners may automatically process information such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, approximate location, and app interaction information.

This information is handled by Google according to Google's own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Permissions

The application uses internet access to load advertisements and may check network availability.

Devashmi Cash Tool does not request access to contacts, precise location, camera, microphone, or financial accounts for its calculation features.

5. Screenshot Sharing

The screenshot-sharing feature is initiated only when you choose to use the Share option.

Screenshots are generated temporarily on your device and are shared only through the application or service selected by you.

Devashmi does not upload or store these screenshots on its own servers.

6. Third-Party Services

The application may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

These services may process information according to their respective terms and privacy policies.

7. Data Security

Devashmi does not operate a server for storing your cash-counting, GST, or discount calculation data.

No digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should use reasonable care when sharing screenshots or calculation results.

8. Children's Privacy

Devashmi Cash Tool is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions, feedback, or support, contact:
